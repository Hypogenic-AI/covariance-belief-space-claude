# Downloaded Papers

## Core Papers (Directly Relevant)

1. **Can Large Language Models Predict Associations Among Human Attitudes?** (`2503.21011_llm_predict_attitude_associations.pdf`)
   - Authors: Ana Ma, Derek Powell (2025)
   - arXiv: 2503.21011
   - Why relevant: Directly demonstrates LLMs capture latent covariance structure of human belief systems. Uses 64 attitudes, finds r=0.77 correlation between LLM-estimated and human attitude correlations.

2. **Whose Opinions Do Language Models Reflect? (OPINIONQA)** (`2503.10649_measuring_political_preferences_ai.pdf`)
   - Note: The OPINIONQA paper is Santurkar et al. 2023 (arXiv:2303.17548), available via the code repo.
   - Authors: Santurkar, Durmus, Ladhak, Lee, Liang, Hashimoto (2023)
   - Why relevant: Introduced OPINIONQA benchmark with 1498 survey questions, foundational dataset.

3. **Measuring Political Preferences in AI Systems** (`2503.10649_measuring_political_preferences_ai.pdf`)
   - Authors: David Rozado (2025)
   - arXiv: 2503.10649
   - Why relevant: Multi-method assessment of LLM political bias, shows structured bias patterns.

4. **Assessing LLMs for Moral Value Pluralism** (`2312.10075_assessing_llms_moral_value_pluralism.pdf`)
   - Authors: Benkler, Mosaphir, Friedman, Smart, Schmer-Galunder (2023, NeurIPS)
   - arXiv: 2312.10075
   - Why relevant: Uses WVS factor analysis to assess LLM values. Traditional-secular factor loadings (0.51-0.70) provide ground truth for validating PCA dimensions.

5. **Language Model Fine-Tuning on Scaled Survey Data** (`2502.16761_lm_finetuning_survey_opinions.pdf`)
   - Authors: Suh et al. (2025)
   - arXiv: 2502.16761
   - Why relevant: Introduces individual belief embeddings — directly related to belief space dimensionality.

6. **Belief Space Planning: A Covariance Steering Approach** (`2105.11092_belief_space_covariance_steering.pdf`)
   - Authors: Zheng, Ridderhof, Tsiotras, Agha-mohammadi (2021)
   - arXiv: 2105.11092
   - Why relevant: Different domain (robotics), but provides mathematical framework for covariance in belief space.
